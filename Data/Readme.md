# Energy Landscapes

Only two files are contained for energy lanscapes:

  1. `dem-projected.tif`: the raster of the Digital Elevation Model (DEM) used to calculate energy landscapes.
  2. `10-maximum.tif`: the raster of calculated energy costs used in downstream analyses.

Both raster have 6659 x 5023 pixels and have in the coordinate reference system PROJ4 `"+proj=laea +lat_0=52 +lon_0=10 +x_0=4321000 +y_0=3210000 +ellps=GRS80 +units=m +no_defs"`.
